What we do

Equal Construction LTD is a private Limited liability corporation that offers world class engineering services from a highly dedicated and competent team of engineers, designers, surveyors and developers. The company provides contractual and consultancy services in structural engineering, road works and electro-mechanical engineering fields.

Working together

We are committed to client satisfaction which we have always achieved through extensive customer support, research in technology evolution as well as immediate response to customer requirements.

Road Construction Works

This involves construction of driveways to complex projects such as highways, bridges, Equal construction adapts its resources and offers its public and private sector customers the techniques and products for example concrete pavements, mastic asphalt and asphalt mixes best suited to their specific requirements.

Structural engineering works

This involves designing the bones and muscles that create the form and shape of man-made structures. Here, structural engineers need to understand and calculate the stability, strength and rigidity of the structures they design. This is integrated with those of other designers such as architects.

Electro mechanical works

This involves both the electrical and the mechanical aspects of an engineering project which include, electric installations, elevator installation, plumbing and so on.
