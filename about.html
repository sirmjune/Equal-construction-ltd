What we do

Equal Construction LTD is a private Limited liability corporation that offers world class engineering services from a highly dedicated and competent team of engineers, designers, surveyors and developers. The company provides contractual and consultancy services in structural engineering, road works and electro-mechanical engineering fields.

Working together

We are committed to client satisfaction which we have always achieved through extensive customer support, research in technology evolution as well as immediate response to customer requirements.

Why choose us

We work with architects and designers to produce beautiful, functional structures. The company strives to undertake reliable, efficient and cost effective projects on both medium and turnkey scales.

Call us today and bring our project management skills and extensive construction experience to your next project.

our vision

To constantly grow the value of our business throughout the East African region with the highest level of customer satisfaction and integrity.

our mission

To provide superior construction and engineering services exceeding customer expectations through our culture of quality work, innovation, integrity, safety, teamwork and timely completion of the works.
